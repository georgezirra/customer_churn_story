Recommendations

1. Onboarding: Making the First Months Count

The first months decide everything. A simple check-in, a quick bill walkthrough, or a welcome guide can turn new customers into long-term ones.

2. Contract nudges: Reward Commitment

Month-to-month customers leave easily. Offer gentle perks ‚Äî like bonus data or a free upgrade ‚Äî to encourage them to stay longer.

3. Billing Fixes: No More Surprises

Unexpected bills drive people away. Spot changes early and give clear options so customers feel in control and cared for.

Operationalize: Making the Model Work for You

Every week, the model spots the customers most likely to leave. The team then reaches out, tests what works best, and feeds the results back in. Over time, it learns, improves, and keeps more customers happy.
